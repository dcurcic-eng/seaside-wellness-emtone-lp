Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

What is cellulite?

Subcutaneous fat pushing against fibrous connective tissue, creating the puckered, dimpled look on the skin's surface.

What does treatment feel like?

Like a hot-stone massage with gentle vibration, comfortably lying down. Most patients find it relaxing.

How many treatments will I need?

Most patients do 4 treatments, 1-2 times per week, with each session lasting about 20 minutes.

How soon will I see results?

Some patients notice a difference after one session, with results continuing to improve over the following months.

Is there any downtime?

None. EMTONE® is non-invasive, so you can return to your normal day right after your session.

Who performs the treatment?

Krista Shenkman, APRN, an experienced provider who personally guides your care from start to finish.
